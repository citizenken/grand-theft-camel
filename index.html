Keys

  • arrows to move
  • Num 1-3 to select trade item
  • T to drop selected trade item
  • Y to cycle through user items
  • Space to use user item

User Items (only sword, sling and water work now)

  • Gray = Sword
  • Black = Sling
  • Brown = Lance
  • White = Empty Water Bag (fill by running into well while selected)
  • Blue = Full Water Bag

Red bar is health, blue is thirst.
If thirst is full, health starts going down. Drink (run into) a well to remove thirst
